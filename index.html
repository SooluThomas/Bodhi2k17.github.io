About CSI

The Computer Society of India Student Branch of Viswajyothi College of Engineering and Technology proudly presents hackOn - 2017. This event could push the envelope of thinking and doing creative ideas that come up from the young hearts and souls of India. hackOn - 2017 is a platform in establishing the innnovative ideas from the specified problem themes.
Be a part of it to explore the better YOU!!!

Schedule

Phase 1

Online Registations and Abstract Submission
Interested participants Team Leader can register their name along with the Team Members and abstract for the selected problem theme.
The last Registration date is 15th July, 2017

Phase 2

Short Listing
From the participants who submited their team details and abstract, finalized top 10 teams will be selected for the Final Round of hackOn - 2017.
The teams who are selected will be informed via e-mail.

Phase 3

Competition Begins
hackOn - 2017 will begin at 9:00am on August 4, 2017 at Research and Development Hall, VJCET. All the short listed team must reach the venue before 8:45am with a registration fee of ₹300 per participant.

Phase 4

Competition Ends
hackOn - 2017 will ends at 9:00am on August 5, 2017. All the short listed team members will get a certificate. Result announcement will be done after evaluation. The result will be published on the same date and the cash prizes distributed.
